Sources of Displayed Data

Topus may display content such as images, headlines, and URLs from public and categorized sources on the app’s Home or Explore screens. These sources include:

  • Google News RSS: For publicly available headlines from trusted media sources.
  • YouTube Trending: For popular videos based on region and topic, retrieved via publicly accessible endpoints or metadata.
  • Dailymotion API: For trending videos based on their public API.
  • Tranco List (https://tranco-list.eu/): To reference and categorize top-ranked websites based on global traffic.

These sources are used for metadata display only (such as title, image, source domain) and always link back to the original platform. We do not embed, store, or redistribute any full content, video stream, or copyrighted material.

If you are a content owner and wish to exclude your website from being referenced in Topus, please contact us at: topuswebbrowser@gmail.com. We will respect takedown or exclusion requests.
